§ 01 — Field notes

I make data tell the truth — then build the AI that acts on it.

For four years I've lived in the space between spreadsheets and strategy. From Wobot.ai in Delhi to Nexford in London and now Euronet in Dubai, the job has been the same at its core: take messy, scattered data and turn it into something a business can confidently decide on.

“Building business with data and AI.”

I'm Google-certified and fluent in Python, SQL, Power BI and Tableau — and just as comfortable inside CRMs like Salesforce, Zoho, HubSpot and Odoo, wiring them together with Zapier.
