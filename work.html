Draftly — an AI writing assistant that sounds like you

A Chrome extension (Manifest V3) that humanizes, replies, translates and fixes grammar right inside Gmail, LinkedIn and WhatsApp — with a bring-your-own-key model for Claude, GPT, Gemini and more. Stack · JavaScript · LLM APIs · Chrome MV3 · UX

The challenge

AI writing tools are powerful but locked in separate tabs, and most send your text to a vendor's servers. People want help where they already write, without surrendering privacy.

What I built

A single floating panel that follows you across the web. Pick a mode (Humanize, Reply, Translate, Grammar), click once, edit, apply. It reads the whole conversation thread to draft replies that fit — and you bring your own API key, so text never routes through Draftly's servers.

JavaScriptChrome MV3OpenAIClaudeGeminiPrompt engineering

The result

A working, open-source product and my hands-on AI-engineering lab — shipping real LLM features to real users.

▲ Open-source · BYO-key · live
